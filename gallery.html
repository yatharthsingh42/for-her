Words I’ve Written for You ✍️

Every moment with you is a beautiful touch on my soul that I wish to feel again.

All these lights and I still beg to see your face among them...

To die in your arms surrounded by memories of us is a death far too sweet.

Blessed be the sun that kisses your skin every morning...

The day I comprehend what you mean to me is the day maybe my brains lay scattered all over this floor.
